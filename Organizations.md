# Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA)
The *IANA* manages both IPv4 and IPv6 addresses and allocates blocks of them to the *Regional Internet Registries (RIR)* 
## Regional Internet Registries (RIR)
RIRs are responsible for allocating IP addresses to ISPs, who provide address blocks to organizations and smaller ISPs.
